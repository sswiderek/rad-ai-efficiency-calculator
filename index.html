How the Rad AI Calculator Works 💡

This calculator estimates your practice's potential time and cost savings by automating radiology report generation with Rad AI. The assumptions are based on real-world industry benchmarks and feedback from Rad AI users.

Steps to Use:

  1. Input Your Practice Details 🏥

    Enter the number of radiologists, average reports per day, time per report, and radiologist salary.

  2. See Your Savings 📊
    • Total Time Saved ⏱
    • Annual Cost Savings 💰
    • Additional Reports Completed 📈

Assumptions Made ⚙️

  • ⏱ Time Saved: The calculator assumes that Rad AI can reduce report completion time by up to 35%, based on industry data indicating that AI-assisted reporting significantly reduces repetitive tasks.
  • 💰 Cost Savings: Savings are calculated based on the average annual salary of a radiologist and the time saved through automation. The average hourly wage is derived from the annual salary divided by 2,080 hours (standard full-time work year).
  • 📅 Working Days Per Year: The calculator assumes 250 working days per year, accounting for weekends and holidays.
  • 📈 Report Volume: Estimates are based on typical daily report volumes for radiologists, which vary based on specialty, practice size, and geographic location.
  • 📊 Scalability: The time and cost savings scale with the size of the practice, meaning larger practices with more radiologists will see higher absolute savings.
